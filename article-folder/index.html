Color as Materials

The articles *Everyday Color Theory,* *Gradients: The Colorful Design Trend Aiming to Soothe These Anxious Times,* and *Color politics of design* explore how color impacts perception, emotion, and culture. Cassidy highlights its subjectivity and cultural ties, Alioto examines gradients as tools for calm in anxious times, and *color politics* emphasizes its historical and societal power. UX designers can learn to use color thoughtfully, balancing emotional impact with cultural awareness to create meaningful and inclusive designs.
